Justin May

-

In 2003 my father was diagnosed with uluvela melanoma, a cancer of the eye. Uluvela melanoma makes it really hard to see. The therapy slowly deteroriates your eyesight over the years, and it had a profound effect on me growing up. My dad always noticed the little things. In a way he had to overcorrect for his vision and see the beauty in the mundane. He always pointed out the views when cross bridges, the rolling hills next to backroads in New Jersey, and the sprawling sunsets at the beach. I've learned to appreciate life so much more.
