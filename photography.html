Who Am I?

I am a sophomore at the Rutgers Engineering Honors Academy, studying Electrical and Computer Enigneering (ECE) and Computer Science. Raised theravada buddhist, I strongly believe in using compassion and kindness to better identify and solve the suffering of those around us. Thus, I believe in utilizing the application of technology for the greater good. All my personal projects have been inspired by problems I or those around me have experienced. Beyond that, I love pursuing my many hobbies: photography, Model UN, longboarding, and reading.
