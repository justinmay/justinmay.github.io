Who Am I?

I am a sophomore at the Rutgers Engineering Honors Academy studying Electrical and Computer Enigneering(ECE) double majored with Computer Science. I am a strong believer in the application of technology for the greater good. Raised theravada buddhist, I strongly believe in using compassion and loving kindness to better identify with and solve the suffering of those around us. All my personal projects have been inspired by problems I or those around me have experience at some point or another. Beyond that, I love pursuing my many hobbies: photography, Model UN, longboarding, and reading.
